AFTER HOURS and SPECIAL EVENTS!

The range is available off hours and for special events. To make arrangements, contact Tony Poovey at 208-430-1217. Please note that a minimum of 24 hours notice is required.

Occasionally the range will close during regular hours for local law enforcement training. Always check the calendar prior to visiting the range.

About

AFTER HOURS and SPECIAL EVENTS!

Rupert Range is a non-profit 501(c)3 and per an evergreen operational agreement, operates the City of Rupert's Outdoor Shooting Complex for the benefit of the public.

  • All board members are volunteers and receive no compensation for their service.
  • The board consists of 5 members.
  • Each board member serves for two years.
  • To serve on that board a number of characteristics are required. Among those characteristics are:
    1. an affable character
    2. knowledge of gun safety
    3. commitment to service
    4. availability
    5. a willingness to work
    6. good business acumen
